In this Backend Project, I have tried to design a online POS System
To do this,
I have used :
1.Php Laravel framework (5.4)
2.jquery
3.bootstrap
4.AJAX
I have used CDN link of jquery,ajax and bootstrap.
Things Done:
1.Create Authenticated User
2.Create Customer with List
3.Create Category with List,Edit
4.Create Product Categorywise with List,Edit
5.Create Invoice page where customers data come from customer page and product data come product page
6.In invoice page Selling price  can be automatically calculated with only quantity and discount of chosen product.
7.Selling price of Product has been used as Price In Invoice page
8.Invoice is saved into database
